What is Dropshipping?

Dropshipping is a retail fulfillment method where a store doesn't keep the products it sells in stock. Instead, when a store sells a product, it purchases the item from a third party and has it shipped directly to the customer. As a result, the merchant never sees or handles the product.


The biggest difference between dropshipping and the standard retail model is that the selling merchant doesn't stock or own inventory. Instead, the merchant purchases inventory as needed from a third party – usually a wholesaler or manufacturer – to fulfill orders.

What is Yawn Tracker?

Yawn Tracker is a web application that allows you to track the amount of yawns you take during the whole day. According to studies, yawning at-least more than 15 times a day is considered a risk due to lack of sleep or a different symptom. Since there are sleep trackers everywhere, I made this application wondering that there are no such things as a yawn tracker.


This web application gave me inspiration after yawning a bunch of times and wondering how many times I did during a day. After that thought, I wanted to track my yawn ever since and that's why I created it.


How does it work?

The way it works is you go to the web application and track on how many yawns you take upon. The green colour measures on the low measures on yawns you can take but the orange and red colour indicates the high number of yawns you took.


You can always track the amount of yawns within your friends and family by connecting them throughout the platform. You just send them a freind invite and you will can track your others yawn in addition to starting a competition. The less yawns you take, the winner be announced at the end of each week. You will be able to win yawn achievements.
